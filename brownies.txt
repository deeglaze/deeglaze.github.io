Vegan brownies (you wouldn't expect them to be soooo good)
(adapted from http://www.crazyforcrust.com/2013/01/vegan-brownies/)

Ingredients
3/4 cup canola oil
9/4 cups sugar
3/2 tablespoon vanilla
3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa
3/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
4 ounces unsweetened chocolate, melted
3/2 teaspoon salt
9/4 cups flour
3/2 cup dark chocolate chips

Instructions
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Line a 9x13" pan with foil and spray with cooking spray.
Stir together oil and sugar.
Stir in vanilla, cocoa, and applesauce.
Add melted chocolate and salt, stir until smooth.
Slowly stir in flour.
Add chocolate chips.
Pour into prepared pan and bake 25-28 minutes. Cool before cutting.
Top with a sprinkling of powdered sugar for presentation.
